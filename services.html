The price we say is the price you pay

The garage-door trade has a scam reputation it mostly earned — bait pricing, mystery fees, pressure to replace what only needed a repair. We built this company to be the boring opposite.

  • Honest ranges, upfront. We tell you the number before we touch the door — over the phone where we can.
  • No $19.99 bait-and-switch. No surprise call-out fee bolted on after the work.
  • Repair before replace. If a $40 part fixes it, we won't sell you a $700 opener.
  • Written quote before any bigger job. In plain language, with no pressure.
